## Writing Regularly

The hardest part of maintaining a blog is writing consistently. A few things that help:

- Write short posts. Not everything needs to be a deep dive.
- Keep drafts. Some posts take weeks to finish — that's fine.
- Write for your past self. What would have been useful to you a year ago?

## On Keeping a Low Bar

The goal of a personal site isn't to go viral. It's to have a place where your thinking lives. Even a short post that explains one thing clearly is worth publishing.
